Data Classification & Handling Policy

Purpose:
Ensure sensitive data is labeled, stored, and accessed appropriately.

Scope:
All business data, structured and unstructured.

Data Classes:
- Confidential (e.g., PCI, PII)
- Internal Use
- Public

Controls:
- PCI data must be encrypted at rest & in transit
- Data access based on need-to-know
- Periodic reviews of file shares, DBs

Compliance:
PCI DSS 3.4, 3.6; SOX data access principles.
