Incident Response Policy

Purpose:
Ensure effective response to security incidents.

Scope:
All information systems and users.

Phases:
- Detection
- Containment
- Eradication
- Recovery
- Post-incident Review

Requirements:
- Incident playbooks tested annually
- 24/7 monitoring by SOC
- Legal and PR involved per severity

Compliance:
PCI DSS 12.10; SOX for evidence preservation.
