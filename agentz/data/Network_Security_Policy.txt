Network Security Policy (PCI & SOX Aligned)

Purpose:
Protect data integrity and confidentiality through secure network architecture.

Scope:
All corporate and cloud networks.

Controls:
- Segmentation of PCI data environments (DMZ, App, Data zones)
- Firewall rules restrict east-west traffic
- All network traffic logged for 90 days
- VLANs and NAC used to enforce least privilege
- IDS/IPS deployed in DMZ and PCI zones

Compliance:
Aligned with PCI DSS 1.2, 1.3; SOX ITGC network controls.
