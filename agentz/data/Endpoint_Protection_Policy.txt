Endpoint Protection Policy

Purpose:
Ensure all endpoints are secure and compliant.

Scope:
All corporate laptops, desktops, and mobile devices.

Controls:
- Full disk encryption (BitLocker, FileVault)
- EDR agents (CrowdStrike/Defender) required
- Device posture checks prior to VPN connection
- Monthly patching enforced via MDM
- Lost/stolen device procedure in place

Compliance:
Meets PCI 5.1.1 and SOX endpoint control requirements.
