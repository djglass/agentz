Access Control Policy

Purpose:
Restrict access to data and systems based on roles.

Scope:
All employees, systems, and services.

Standards:
- RBAC enforced across AD, AzureAD, and IAM
- MFA required for all admin accounts
- Quarterly access reviews
- Terminated employees disabled within 24h

Compliance:
PCI DSS 7.x, 8.x; SOX ITGC on logical access controls.
